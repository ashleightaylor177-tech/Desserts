About Us

What Makes Our Desserts Special?

Founded in 2010, Borcelle's Pancake started as a small family bakery with a big dream: to create the most delightful, visually stunning, and delicious desserts that would bring smiles to our customers' faces.

Our secret is in our commitment to using only the finest ingredients, traditional methods combined with innovative techniques, and most importantly, infusing every creation with love and passion.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of happy customers, each dessert crafted with the same care and attention that marked our very first creations.

Our Journey

2010

Borcelle's Pancake opened its doors as a small neighborhood bakery with just three employees.

2013

We introduced our signature pink theme and expanded our menu to include custom celebration cakes.

2015

Opened our second location and launched online ordering, making our desserts accessible to more customers.

2018

Won the "Best Dessert Shop" award from the National Culinary Association for three consecutive years.

2021

Expanded to include a dessert catering service for special events and corporate functions.

2023

Launched our recipe blog and started offering virtual baking classes to dessert enthusiasts worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes! We have an expanding selection of gluten-free and vegan desserts. Please check our menu or contact us for current options. We take extra precautions to prevent cross-contamination for those with severe allergies.

How far in advance should I place an order for a custom cake?

We recommend placing orders for custom cakes at least 72 hours in advance. For elaborate wedding cakes or large orders, we suggest placing your order 2-3 weeks ahead of time to ensure availability.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 15-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees vary based on distance. We also offer nationwide shipping for certain non-perishable items.

Can I visit your bakery in person?

Absolutely! We'd love to welcome you to our bakery. We have seating available for enjoying desserts on-site, and our staff would be happy to give you a tour of our kitchen (with advance notice).

Ready to Taste Our Creations?

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply satisfying your sweet tooth, we have the perfect dessert for you. Visit us today or place your order online.
